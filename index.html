Journey is a church that believes in the message of Jesus, the power of his church, and the ability of his people to change the world.

As you look around, we hope you’ll discover Journey Church isn’t a building or a religious institution, rather we’re a diverse group of PEOPLE seeking to know God and discover the amazing life He designed us to live. We’ll be the first ones to admit we don’t have it all figured out. However, we have discovered some truths that have totally transformed us and we’d love to share them with you. Thanks for letting us come alongside you as you seek to navigate your life’s journey.
