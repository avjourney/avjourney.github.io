Current Job Listings - Updated March 31, 2016

Children's and Family Ministry Pastor - Full Time

Journey Church is seeking a new Children's and Family Ministry leader to take our ministry to the next level. What does that mean? Someone with the skills and passion to take an already effective weekly program for nursery, preschoolers, and elementary students and transform it into a comprehensive strategy that partners with parents and other ministries to grow and equip our future generation of church leaders.

Presently, we host a nursery, preschool, and children's worship program during our two morning worship celebrations. A new leader will continue to develop the present ministry, assist in expanding the program to include a third service beginning Fall 2016, and develop new strategies to strengthen families and equip parents with tools to disciple their children. Contact us below, or send a resume to todd@avjourney.com.

Facilities Manager - Part Time

Journey Church is looking for a part-time Facilities Manager. The Facilities Manager would work 20-25 hours per week. Responsibilities include cleaning the facility on a weekly basis, setting up rooms for various events, grounds care, minor maintenance projects, and managing church supplies. The Facilities Manager would be required to work either Sunday afternoon or Monday morning along with time spent on Saturday evenings in preparation for next day services. The balance of hours will be spread throughout the week as needed.

Note: Candidates must have a flexible schedule that allows the freedom to be available when needed. The ideal candidate should be self-motivated, has a strong work ethic, and has a good eye for identifying what needs to be done to maintain a high level of excellence.

To receive more information, and apply for the position, fill out the form below and we will contact you with more details.
