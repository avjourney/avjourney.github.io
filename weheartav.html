#WEHEARTAV

Ya, we heard about the recent survey that listed Lancaster and Palmdale as some of the worst places to live in L.A. County due to factors like crime, drugs, poverty, homelessness, etc... but you know what?

WE LOVE THE AV!

Yep, just like most of you, we believe that home is what you make it. So, as for us, we want to make this the most amazing community possible!

In case we haven't met, we are the people of Journey Church. No perfect people here, just a bunch of regular folks who have come to believe that there is an awesome life awaiting all of us. We admit up front that we don't have all the answers, but we have discovered that there truly is a way of living that unleashes our greatest potential as human beings. A way of life that sets us free from the very things that cause us to live less than our dreams. It's that beautiful life that we want to share with our valley.

Maybe you're not much for church, but really want the best life possible. That's exactly what we want too! So how about joining us as we embark on this breathtaking Journey! If you'd like to know more about us first, feel free to browse our page, watch our videos, listen to a podcast, or even email us if you have specific questions (info@avjourney.com). But if you really want to know what we're all about, please come visit us.

Again, in case we weren't clear, WEHEARTAV, and we invite you to join us in making this the BEST PLACE POSSIBLE TO LIVE!

A recent study listed Palmdale and Lancaster as the worst of the worst communities in Los Angeles County. With high levels of poverty, crime, drug use, and homelessness, our valley has a lot of discouraging issues. It would be easy to throw up our hands and give up. Or we can be the CHANGE our valley really needs.

We love the Antelope Valley. That's the heartbeat of Journey Church. There are half a million people living in the valley. Many of them are searching for answers to some of life's deepest questions. We know the answer is a relationship with Jesus.

For the next six weeks, we want to blitz the valley with the message that we love them and, more importantly, God loves them too. The entire blitz leads up to Easter Sunday where we will share the life-changing message of Jesus.
